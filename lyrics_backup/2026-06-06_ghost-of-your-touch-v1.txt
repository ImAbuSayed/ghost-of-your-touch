[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I still feel your fingers tracing down my spine
A midnight craving and a sweet red wine
You left a mark that I cannot erase
A burning heat in a quiet place

[Verse 1]
The dust is settling on the wooden floor
I see your shadow dancing by the door
A fading fire and a restless tune
I trace your outline by the silver moon

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath is a heavy sigh
Every glance at the starlit sky
Pulls me back to the warmth we knew
I am starving for a taste of you

[Chorus]
Oh, the ghost of your touch is lingering still
A sweet slow poison, a fevered thrill
I close my eyes and I pull you near
The ghost of your touch is living here

[Post-Chorus]
The ghost of your touch is everywhere
[Echo] (Everywhere)

[Verse 2]
The tangled sheets are lying on the bed
I replay every dirty word you said
Your gentle whisper like a summer breeze
Is bringing this aching soul to my knees

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every heartbeat is beating fast
Caught inside of a perfect past
I am falling without a net
You are a feeling I won't forget

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]

[Echo] (Everywhere)

[Bridge]
